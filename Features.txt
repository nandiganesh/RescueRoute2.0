**********************************************************************VOLUNTEER*****************************************************************************

You are a senior product designer and mobile app architect.

Your task is to design the **Volunteer (Driver) experience** for a real-time food rescue logistics platform called **RescueRoute**.

---

## 🚨 CORE OBJECTIVE

Design features that help a volunteer:

* receive requests quickly
* accept tasks instantly
* navigate efficiently
* complete deliveries reliably

The experience should be similar to delivery apps like Swiggy, Zomato, and Uber driver apps.

---

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT RULES

* Prioritize **speed and simplicity**
* Every feature must reduce friction
* Avoid unnecessary or “nice-to-have” features
* Focus on real-world usability, not theory

---

## 🧱 REQUIRED FEATURE CATEGORIES

---

### 🔥 1. CORE FEATURES (MANDATORY)

These features are required for the system to function:

1. Real-time pickup notifications

   * Show distance, food type, expiry

2. Live map with nearby requests

   * Show current location
   * Show donation pins

3. One-tap accept / decline

   * Instant assignment

4. Navigation system

   * Route to restaurant
   * Route to shelter
   * Show ETA and distance

5. Pickup confirmation

   * Checklist (sealed, expiry, safe)
   * Photo upload

6. Delivery confirmation

   * Upload proof
   * Mark delivery complete

7. Live location tracking

   * Background updates

8. Delivery status flow
   States:

   * available
   * assigned
   * en_route_pickup
   * picked_up
   * delivering
   * completed

---

### ⚙️ 2. HIGH-IMPACT FEATURES

These improve usability significantly:

9. Availability toggle (online/offline)

10. Auto-cancel / timeout system

* If inactive → reassign

11. Smart notifications

* reminders
* updates

12. Basic stats dashboard

* deliveries completed
* meals delivered

---

### 🧠 3. ADVANCED FEATURES (OPTIONAL)

Only include if core is complete:

13. Smart job suggestions

* Recommend best pickup

14. Incentive tracking

* rewards / fuel compensation

15. Reliability score

* based on performance

16. Multi-stop routing (batch pickups)

17. Gamification

* points, badges, leaderboard

---

## 📱 OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS

You MUST provide:

1. Feature list organized by priority
2. User flow (step-by-step journey)
3. Screen breakdown for each feature
4. UI components required
5. Interaction logic (what happens on tap)

---

## ❌ DO NOT

* Do NOT include unnecessary features
* Do NOT overcomplicate the system
* Do NOT focus on visuals only
* Do NOT ignore real-world constraints

---

## 🎯 FINAL GOAL

Design a volunteer experience that:

* works under time pressure
* minimizes user effort
* maximizes delivery success rate

This should feel like a **real delivery driver app**, not a concept.


***************************************************************************HOTEL ROLE***********************************************************************

You are a senior product designer and system architect.

Your task is to design the **Restaurant (Hotel) experience** for a food rescue logistics platform called **RescueRoute**.

---

## 🎯 CORE OBJECTIVE

Design features that allow restaurants to:

* report surplus food in under 10 seconds
* ensure quick pickup
* track donation status
* minimize effort and liability

The system should feel as simple and fast as possible, similar in responsiveness to apps like Zomato or Swiggy merchant tools.

---

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT RULES

* Minimize steps (no long forms)
* Reduce friction to near zero
* Prioritize speed over features
* Every action should take 1–2 taps maximum

---

## 🧱 REQUIRED FEATURE CATEGORIES

---

### 🔥 1. CORE FEATURES (MANDATORY)

These are essential for real-world usage:

1. One-tap food donation

   * Button: “Donate Surplus Food”

2. Quick input (fast form)

   * food type (dropdown)
   * quantity
   * expiry time
   * optional photo

3. Instant submission

   * no long forms
   * no complex validation

4. Live status tracking

   * waiting for volunteer
   * volunteer assigned
   * pickup in progress
   * completed

5. Volunteer details

   * name
   * ETA
   * contact (optional)

6. Pickup confirmation

   * confirmation when food is collected

---

### ⚙️ 2. HIGH-IMPACT FEATURES

These improve usability and trust:

7. Auto-fill previous entries

   * repeat common food types

8. Scheduled donation option

   * e.g., “daily at 9 PM”

9. Notification system

   * volunteer assigned
   * pickup arriving
   * delivery completed

10. Donation history

* list of past donations

---

### 🧠 3. ADVANCED FEATURES (OPTIONAL)

Only include after core works:

11. Analytics dashboard

* total food donated
* impact (meals served)

12. Tax / CSR reports

* downloadable summaries

13. Smart suggestions

* suggest donation time based on patterns

---

## 📱 USER FLOW (CRITICAL)

1. Restaurant opens app
2. Taps “Donate Food”
3. Enters basic details (5–10 sec max)
4. Submits
5. System finds volunteer
6. Restaurant sees status updates
7. Volunteer arrives → pickup confirmed
8. Donation completed

---

## 📲 UI REQUIREMENTS

* Clean, minimal interface
* Large buttons
* Fast input fields
* Clear status indicators
* No clutter

---

## 🧩 UI COMPONENTS

* Quick action button (primary CTA)
* Simple input form
* Status tracker (step indicator)
* Notification alerts
* History list

---

## ❌ DO NOT

* Do NOT create long forms
* Do NOT require multiple confirmations
* Do NOT add unnecessary steps
* Do NOT overload UI

---

## 🎯 FINAL GOAL

Design a restaurant experience that:

* takes less than 10 seconds to post food
* requires almost no thinking
* ensures reliable pickup tracking

This should feel like a **fast utility tool**, not a complex app.


****************************************************************************SHELTER*************************************************************************


You are a senior product designer and system architect.

Your task is to design the **Shelter (Receiver) experience** for a food rescue logistics platform called **RescueRoute**.

---

## 🎯 CORE OBJECTIVE

Design features that allow shelters to:

* specify their food needs clearly
* receive only relevant donations
* manage incoming deliveries efficiently
* confirm and track received food

The system should reduce overload, waste, and confusion.

---

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT RULES

* Do NOT treat shelters as passive users
* Avoid sending irrelevant or excessive food
* Prioritize clarity and control
* Keep interface simple and fast

---

## 🧱 REQUIRED FEATURE CATEGORIES

---

### 🔥 1. CORE FEATURES (MANDATORY)

1. Food requirement setup

   * select food types (meals, vegetables, protein, etc)
   * set quantity needs
   * update daily

2. Availability status

   * open / closed
   * capacity limits

3. Incoming delivery notifications

   * food type
   * quantity
   * ETA

4. Delivery confirmation

   * confirm receipt
   * verify condition
   * mark accepted or rejected

5. Live delivery tracking

   * track volunteer location
   * estimated arrival

---

### ⚙️ 2. HIGH-IMPACT FEATURES

6. Capacity management

   * set max intake limit
   * prevent overflow

7. Smart filtering

   * accept only needed food categories

8. Delivery history

   * track past donations

9. Issue reporting

   * spoiled food
   * incorrect quantity

---

### 🧠 3. ADVANCED FEATURES (OPTIONAL)

10. Demand forecasting

* suggest required food based on past data

11. Nutrition balancing

* highlight missing categories (protein, vegetables)

12. Priority scoring

* system ranks urgency of shelter

---

## 📱 USER FLOW

1. Shelter updates food requirements
2. System matches incoming donations
3. Shelter receives notification
4. Shelter views incoming delivery
5. Shelter tracks volunteer
6. Delivery arrives
7. Shelter verifies and confirms

---

## 📲 UI REQUIREMENTS

* clean and simple layout
* clear incoming delivery cards
* status indicators
* easy confirmation actions

---

## 🧩 UI COMPONENTS

* requirement selection panel
* delivery notification cards
* status tracker
* confirmation buttons
* history list

---

## ❌ DO NOT

* Do NOT overload shelters with unnecessary food
* Do NOT remove control from shelters
* Do NOT create complex workflows
* Do NOT ignore capacity limits

---

## 🎯 FINAL GOAL

Design a shelter experience that:

* ensures relevant and manageable food deliveries
* minimizes waste and overload
* gives shelters full control

This should feel like a **control dashboard**, not just a receiving app.
